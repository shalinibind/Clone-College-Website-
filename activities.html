Student Clubs

The college has various platforms in the fields of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities including social service for the overall development of the students. Many clubs / forums are active on the GPREC campus, initiated by enthusiastic students with a passion for the arts, culture or hobbies - and keep students occupied after academic hours.

  • IEI Students' chapter for each department - conducts seminars on technical topics, quizzes, lectures etc.
  • Bhuvana Vijayam - The literary and cultural association provides a platform to the students to exhibit their talents in the fields of culture and literature.
  • Center for Awareness & Discussion – working with the objective of familiarizing the students with current topics through group discussions and quizzes.
  • Vivekananda Study circle – working with the objectives of universal brotherhood, selfless service to the society and inculcating noble values in youth.
  • Art of Living Forum – started to bring joy, a stress free mind, healthy body and blossomed life to students as well as faculty of the college.
  • T&P club – started to create awareness among students about career opportunities, training needs and industry expectations.
